What is SAP IBP?

SAP Integrated Business Planning (SAP IBP) is a cloud-based supply chain planning solution that connects sales, finance, and operations in one unified platform. It enables organizations to plan collaboratively across the supply chain — from demand sensing and forecasting to inventory optimization and supply network planning — all enhanced with embedded AI and machine learning.

Exception Management Agent

Beta ShippedPlanning Assistant
Supply Network PlannerLisa

The Exception Management Agent parses planning alerts in IBP, performs root-cause analysis, and surfaces insights and resolution options with reasoning. Planners stay in control of decisions; the agent removes the search, correlation and triage burden. Over time, within planner-defined guardrails, the agent auto-resolves routine alerts and escalates only those needing real human judgment.

Exception Detection & Classification

Parses alerts by business impact so planners focus only on what matters.

Root-Cause Analysis

Reads master data, key figures and gating factors to explain why each exception fired.

Resolution Options & Decision Guidance

Surfaces resolution options with reasoning, trade-offs and full supporting data.

Workflow & Task Orchestration

Stages scenarios, captures planner confirmation, keeping the human in the loop.

Need

  • Planners spend hours triaging hundreds of alerts to find the few that matter.
  • Diagnosing why an alert fired requires manual lookup across multiple planning views.
  • Decisions rely on gut feel and approximations under tight deadlines.
  • Resolution actions are person-dependent, slow, and inconsistent across the team.

Agent

  • Parses alerts and classifies them by business impact, so planners focus only on what matters.
  • Reads master data, key figures and gating factors to explain why each exception fired.
  • Surfaces resolution options with reasoning, trade-offs and full supporting data.
  • Stages scenarios, captures planner confirmation / learns, keeping the human in the loop.
